HPLtoPIN allows you to take a Bonanza Hand Picked List and turn it into a Pinterest Board filled with pins of your favorite Bonanza items!

To get started you simply need to log in to Pinterest and authorize us to Pin on your behalf.

Once authorized you will

  1. Enter the URL of the Hand Picked List
  2. Confirm the board title and items to Pin

Simple as that your Bonanza items will be Pinned to your Pinterest account
